Quest Brief Story

V has been tasked by Regina Jones to investigate suspicious activities at an abandoned Arasaka warehouse in the industrial district of Watson. Recent netrunner reports indicate that a rogue AI might have taken control of the facility's security systems, trapping several scavengers inside.

Regina believes there's more to this situation than meets the eye - intelligence suggests the warehouse may be storing experimental cybernetic implants that never made it to market due to their extreme side effects.

Your mission is to infiltrate the facility, neutralize the AI threat, rescue any survivors (if interested), and recover information about the experimental technology for Regina's clients.

[ADDITIONAL INFORMATION REDACTED BY ARASAKA SECURITY PROTOCOLS]

The mission follows a three-act structure with branching paths:

  1. Act 1: Infiltration - Player must find a way into the warehouse by either hacking the security systems, finding an alternative entrance, or going in guns blazing.
  2. Act 2: Investigation - Player explores the warehouse, encounters hostile security systems, discovers logs about the experiments, and finds survivors.
  3. Act 3: Confrontation - Player must deal with the rogue AI by either destroying it, containing it, or uploading a virus.

Player choices affect:

  • Survivor fate
  • Regina's trust
  • Future side quests
  • Available loot and rewards

The core gameplay loop consists of:

  1. Exploration - Navigate through the warehouse while avoiding or disabling security systems.
  2. Combat Encounters - Deal with security drones, turrets, and hostile scavengers.
  3. Hacking Challenges - Breach into terminals to gather information and control systems.
  4. Decision Making - Choose how to deal with survivors and the AI.
  5. Resource Management - Limited ammunition and healing items increase tension.

Player skills and attributes will influence available approaches:

  • High Intelligence - More hacking options
  • High Body - Force open locked doors, withstand more damage
  • High Technical Ability - Disable security systems, craft items
  • High Cool - Stealth approach, better dialog options

The warehouse features multiple floors and sections:

  • Reception Area - Initial entry point with basic security
  • Storage Halls - Large open areas with shipping containers and crates
  • Research Labs - Where the experimental implants were developed
  • Server Room - Heart of the facility, where the AI core is located
  • Executive Offices - Contains valuable corporate information
[DESIGNER NOTES SKETCH PLACEHOLDER]

Key design notes:

  • All narrative threads lead to the server room but offer different gameplay experiences
  • AI personality based on psychological profiles of Arasaka executives (unstable, paranoid)
  • Environmental storytelling through found PDAs, employee logs, and security footage
  • Dynamic weather affects visibility and electrical systems
  • Time of day influences guard rotations and security response times

Quest Opening - Regina Jones

REGINA: "V, got something that might be up your alley. Abandoned Arasaka warehouse in Watson's industrial zone. Been quiet for years until my surveillance picked up some unusual activity."

V (Option 1): "What kind of unusual activity?"

REGINA: "Security systems suddenly reactivated after years of dormancy. Then I intercepted distress calls from some scavs who broke in looking for tech to strip. They went silent mid-transmission."

V (Option 2): "What's in it for me, Regina?"

REGINA: "Besides my everlasting gratitude? How about exclusive access to experimental Arasaka tech that never hit the market? My sources say they were working on neural implants with capabilities beyond anything commercially available. Could be worth a fortune to the right buyer."

V (Option 3): "Sounds dangerous. Who else knows about this?"

REGINA: "Far as I know, just the scavs who went in and never came out. Arasaka seems to have forgotten this place exists - or they want it to look that way. Either possibility should concern you."
