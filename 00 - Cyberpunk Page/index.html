Quest Brief Story

THE GHOST IN THE MACHINE

Regina Jones has intel on an abandoned Arasaka warehouse in Watson where experimental tech has mysteriously reactivated after years of dormancy.

Primary Objective: Infiltrate the facility and extract data from the mainframe about Project Chimera.

Secondary Objective: Locate and retrieve the prototype implant if possible.

WARNING: Intelligence suggests the security systems have been compromised by an unknown rogue AI.

Current access level: INSUFFICIENT
Required clearance: OBTAINED - proceed with caution.

[ADDITIONAL INFORMATION REDACTED BY ARASAKA SECURITY PROTOCOLS]

MISSION STRUCTURE & FLOW

Mission flow divided into 3 distinct phases:

PHASE 1: INFILTRATION

• Multiple entry points: Roof access, Service entrance, Main entrance

• Player choice-driven stealth mechanics with hacking alternatives

• Environmental storytelling reveals facility history

PHASE 2: DISCOVERY

• Central lab area protected by automated security systems

• Dialogue with the rogue AI reveals corporate conspiracy

• Player decision: Align with Arasaka remnants or the rogue AI

PHASE 3: EXTRACTION

• High-intensity escape sequence with waves of security drones

• Choice-based ending determined by player allegiance

• Multiple epilogues affect Watson district and Regina's future missions
