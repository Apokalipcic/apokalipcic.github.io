Course Overview

This comprehensive bootcamp provided me with a deep understanding of game mechanics design, taught by industry veteran Alexander Brazie, former designer at Blizzard Entertainment. The course covered advanced techniques for creating engaging gameplay systems, balancing methodologies, and player psychology principles.

Through a combination of theoretical learning and practical applications, I developed skills in creating robust game systems that are both engaging and balanced. The hands-on approach allowed me to apply concepts immediately through design exercises and projects.
