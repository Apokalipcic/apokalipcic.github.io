Quest Brief Story

Cyberpsycho Sighting: Mirror's Edge

Act 1: The Setup

The quest begins with Regina Jones contacting V about another potential cyberpsycho sighting. However, she warns V that "this one is not like the others" and advises them to keep their guard up. Upon arriving at the location, V discovers dead Arasaka combat team members scattered throughout the corridor. This unusual sight prompts Johnny Silverhand to express suspicion about the true nature of this mission.

As V investigates further, they find a dead soldier positioned in a peculiar way, holding a neural chip that seems almost deliberately placed to be found. Despite the suspicious circumstances, V decides to insert the chip, leading Johnny to mock their decision. Shortly after, Rei reveals herself, though not completely – appearing as a spider-like creature crawling on the ceiling.

When Rei drops down to attack V, they attempt to fight back, only to discover that any damage dealt reflects back to themselves. While Johnny theorizes that the chip might be protecting its owner, Rei suddenly stops her assault and retreats into hiding. And new UI Interface appears for V, claiming him as an Owner of Mirror’s Edge chip - revealing the cycling nature of the Chip.

Act 2: The Hunt

The second act transforms into a hide-and-seek game between V and Rei. Throughout the level, V can discover scattered data shards and pieces of information that reveal Rei's backstory. The environment is equipped with flashbangs and hackable light sources that V can utilize.

When V manages to reduce Rei's health to 30-40%, she retreats. V pursues her, only to find her in a vulnerable position as the Mirror's Edge chip malfunctions and burns out, with its UI effect disappearing completely. V attempts to communicate with Rei, but she cannot respond verbally due to being mute. Instead, she communicates through the gestures of her spider legs, either opening or closing them.

V recognizes her as a victim of Arasaka and has the choice to either help her or attempt to eliminate her. During this moment, V discovers an alert on a nearby working PC warning of approaching Arasaka forces. While V is distracted by this information, Rei takes the opportunity to escape.

Act 3: The Resolution

The final act splits into two possible paths based on V's earlier choice:

Alliance Path

If V chooses to help Rei, they fight alongside each other against the Arasaka forces, though in separate areas. The quest concludes with V calling Viktor to come and help Rei. This path results in V losing Regina's reward and some reputation with her.

Elimination Path

If V chooses to eliminate Rei, the situation evolves into a three-way combat between V, Rei, and the Arasaka forces. The final confrontation becomes a straightforward one-on-one fight with an injured Rei, without chip limitations and with access to V's full arsenal. This path ends with V receiving Regina's reward and gaining reputation with her.

GAMEPLAY LOOP

DISCOVERY
Phase 1

Player uncovers the unique mirror mechanic through gameplay. Combat encounters demonstrate how V's attacks reflect back onto themselves, teaching players to adapt their strategy.

ADAPTATION
Phase 2

Player must utilize environmental elements to deal with Rei indirectly. This includes hacking light sources, using flashbangs, and navigating the environment strategically.

RESOLUTION
Phase 3

Player's moral choice determines the final approach. Either cooperate with Rei against Arasaka forces, or eliminate her when she's vulnerable, each leading to different rewards and consequences.
